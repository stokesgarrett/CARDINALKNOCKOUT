ABOUT

As part of our Cyber-Security training program here at UIW, Cardinal Knockout was created by our own Cyber-Security students here at the university. The purpose of Cardinal Knockout is to help test and improve your cyber knowledge. In today's age, cyber-attacks are becoming more and more prevalent, and it is important to have a good grasp of possible attacks in order to stay safe. No matter what field you dabble in, there is always a cyber-security aspect involved, and your information deserves to be protected!

HOW TO PLAY

To play Cardinal Knockout, Simply click on the "Let's Play" at the top of the page, and you can begin playing the game. Only UIW students and Employees are eligible to recieve coupon rewards. The game can be played from your desktop, laptop, or mobile device.
